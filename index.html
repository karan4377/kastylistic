My Design Process

1

Discover and Research

My first step in logo design is to discuss with the client. This way I can explore how the client feels about their business and the awesome work that they are doing in words that go beyond the official corporate statement.

2

Sketch

I bring in my ideas on the page and start to narrow down my concepts. I use a traditional pen/pencil to sketch different possible designs. My goal by the end is to have 1-3 solid concepts to explore further in the next step.

3

Design

Once I have a few strong logo ideas, then I just need to use Adobe Illustrator to design vector graphics. Here I simply recreate those sketches on the computer and then design multiple variations of each idea to arrive at the best option.

4

Presentation

Now is the time to present my chosen concept in different possible arrangements. I like to show my work to clients as high-resolution jpeg files or one multiple-page high-resolution pdf. In this step, I will also take the client's feedback.

5

Color and Refine

Color can finally come into play when the client has approved my concept. In this step, I will also refine the logo according to the client's feedback. I will again present the design to the client for final approval.

6

Prepare and Deliver

Once the client approves the final refined version, I have now finished the logo design process. I will prepare the design and supply the client with final files like vector, raster, and digital versions like jpg and png of each logo variation.
